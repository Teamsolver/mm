Welcome to Help section

How can we assist you?


At MuseMate, we aim to make your experience as seamless as possible. If you have any questions or need assistance, you’re in the right place! Here’s a guide to help you navigate our website and services.
1. Booking Museum tickets

> How do I book tickets?
Use our chatbot to find your desired museum. Simply type the museum’s name or browse suggestions.
Once you’ve selected a museum, the chatbot will guide you through the available time slots and ticket options.
Choose the number of tickets and complete the payment using our secure checkout.
> Can I book for multiple museums at once?
Yes, you can add multiple museums to your cart and complete a single checkout for all your bookings.

2. Payment Options

> What payment methods are available?
We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, and mobile wallets. Payment is secure and encrypted to protect your information.
> Why was my payment declined?
Check if:
Your card details are correct.
Your card has sufficient funds.
Your bank isn’t blocking the transaction. If issues persist, please contact your bank or try another payment method.

3. Using the Chatbot
> How do I interact with the chatbot?
Simply type your question or request, and our chatbot will respond instantly. It can help with:
Searching for museums
Checking ticket availability
Providing museum details (timings, location, etc.)
Booking tickets
> What if the chatbot doesn't understand my request?
If the chatbot is unable to assist, you can reach out to our customer support team via email or phone.

4. Cancellations and Refunds

> Can I cancel my booking?
Yes, bookings can be canceled up to 24 hours before the scheduled visit. Please visit the “My Bookings” section and click on “Cancel.”
> Will I receive a refund for my canceled booking?
Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days after cancellation. However, some museums may charge a small cancellation fee. You will be notified of any such fees during the cancellation process.

5. Museum Information

> Where can I find information about museums?
Our chatbot provides detailed information about museums, including opening hours, location, and available exhibits. You can also browse the museum profiles on our website.
